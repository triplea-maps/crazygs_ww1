The goal of this map is to create a balanced game, using the history of the first world war as inspiration.

Special Features:
Several "new" weapons that showcase lesser known features.
Minor nations will join the war as the game goes on.
The Kiel Canal, in German controlled Denmark connects sea zones 27 and 51.
Railroads and trains play an important role in this conflict!
Air battles occur before land combat. Aircraft boost land unit strength
Airships can bomb factories. Recon Planes can escort or intercept those bombing raids.
